Spotlight

The Botanic Garden is now recruiting volunteers to train to lead tours for groups of school children and adults. Volunteers also develop thematic tours, staff our reception area, provide hospitality for our special events, and assist with exhibitions.

The New Volunteer 2018 three-day training session runs January 17, 18 & 19 from 9:00 am to 4:00 pm. Registration is required.
If you are interested in becoming a Botanic Garden Volunteer, please fill out a volunteer application and contact Madelaine Zadik.
